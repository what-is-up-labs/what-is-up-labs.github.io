Games With Strangers

 

Use your NFC-enabled Android smartphone to play a suite of games. Send a game to your friend by tapping his or her phone. No friends near? No worries! Just pick up or leave a game at any Games With Strangers tag! Games With Strangers is an app created by Matt Grasser, Nick Ihm & Emily ! Duong, made for TAPPED: The NFC Accelerator in New York City, and won 3rd place! Stay tuned for updates!
